Pathways to a Better Future

What Needs to Happen.

Whats most important to recognize across all factors is the lack of data. Many facts and figures we have shared with you included the phrase "estimated". The reality is, none of these data centers are required or incentivized to report any of these figures, especially in the United States. There are hardly any laws and regulations specifically targeting data centers, and those that do often leave out crypto mining facilities, which likely are even worse on many fronts. Why would these data centers report how many resources they are using and how much potential damage they are causing if they don't have to?

We need reporting.

Mandated, not optional.

If these data centers are not required to report statistics such as energy consumption, water consumption, and sourcing for these resources, we will never have a clear and accurate picture of what is going on. Studies on environmental effects are sparse because there is no data to work from.

We need regulations.

Keep it controlled.

Using the data we find from mandated reporting, restrictions need to be put in place. We can all agree that modern AI, one of the largest contributors to data center growth, has incredible benefits. But if the growth and draw of these data centers aren't kept in check, there might not be a world left to use them in.

We need you.

Yes, you.

These data centers are being operated by billion dollar companies who will spend as much money as needed to lobby against everything mentioned above. Their profit is dependent on our future environments suffering. Without every voice sharing their views with friends and family and contacting their local representatives, this change cannot happen.
