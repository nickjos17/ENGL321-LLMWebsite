Air

  • Stat 1: Data centers and energy production associated with them were estimated to produce 105 million tons of C02.
  • Stat 2: That is more than 2% of all US emissions.
  • Stat 3: Critically, emissions cannot be taken back. Unneeded emissions will continue to raise the ppm of CO2 in our atmosphere.

Noise

  • Stat 1: Constant loud noise has raised sleep and health concerns from locals near Data Centers.
  • Stat 2: This noise pollution also can affect natural wildlife patterns.
  • Stat 3: Noise pollution from data centers is understudied, but we know that Data Centers produce harmful levels of sound.
