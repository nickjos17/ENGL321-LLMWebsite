Electricity

  • Stat 1: In 2023, the United States consumed an estimated 176 tera-watt hours of electricity.
  • Stat 2: Thats enough electricity to power 17 million households.
  • Stat 3: Alternatively, that electricity could be used to convert 25% of all gas vehicles to electric.

Water

  • Stat 1: Water consumption has a similar problem, with estimated annual water consumption of data centers equating to over a million households.
  • Stat 2: Water consumed by data centers is over 50% potable water.
  • Stat 3: Unlike electricity, water is not renewable. We only have so much potable water and data centers just keep drinking.
